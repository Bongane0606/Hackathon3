Frequently Asked Questions

How does the QR code system work?

Each client receives a unique QR code card. When they visit, you simply scan the code using our mobile app or web dashboard, and their visit is automatically recorded. No apps or accounts needed for your clients!

What if my clients don't have smartphones?

We offer SMS-based tracking as an alternative. Clients can text your dedicated salon number, and their visit will be recorded just like with the QR code system.

How are rewards delivered to clients?

When a client reaches the required number of visits, they automatically receive an SMS with their reward details. You can also choose to print the reward directly from your dashboard at checkout.

Can I customize the rewards program?

Absolutely! You can set how many visits are required for rewards, choose between percentage discounts or free services, and even create tiered reward systems for your most loyal clients.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment?

No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with just a few clicks in your dashboard.
